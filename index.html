Water Equilibrium App

The Hallopeau and Dubin method allows to quantitatively characterise a water’s free CO2 content and its fraction that attacks limestone, to calculate the amounts of neutralisation reagents and to anticipate water properties after applying a correction to adjust the pH to the calcium-carbonate balance value.

This App allows to calculate the calcium-carbonate balance using Hallopeau and Dubin method and graph.
